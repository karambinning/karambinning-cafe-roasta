About the café

An honest plate of food on Tower Street.

Cafe Roasta has been feeding King's Lynn from a corner of Tower Street for years — counter-service, plates piled high, nothing precious. Walk in, pick what looks good from the display, and find a table inside or out under the awning.

The kitchen leans traditional British: full English breakfasts laid down from eight in the morning; jacket potatoes, soups and salads through the middle of the day; cakes baked in-house; and on Sundays the roast — beef, pork and turkey with all the trimmings — that the regulars come back for.

Children welcome, dogs welcome at the outside tables, coach parties by arrangement. Vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free options on the counter most days.

What's on

From the counter, all day.

  • Full English breakfast

    Served from 8am through to closing — bacon, sausage, egg, beans, tomato, mushroom, hash brown, toast. Smaller and vegetarian versions on the board.

    from open

  • Sunday roast

    Choose beef, pork or turkey (or a plate of all three). Roast potatoes, Yorkshire pudding, seasonal veg, proper gravy. Vegetarian roast on request.

    Sundays · 9 till 4

  • Jackets, soups & sandwiches

    Daily soup, jacket potatoes with classic fillings, hot & cold sandwiches, salad bowls — the lunchtime backbone of the café.

    lunch

  • Home-made cakes & bakes

    Sponges, traybakes, scones and the day's specials — all baked on site. Take a slice with a pot of tea, or a whole cake to go.

    all day

  • Tea, coffee & soft drinks

    Proper pot of tea, espresso-based coffees, hot chocolates and the usual cold drinks. Take-away cups available at the counter.

    all day

  • Take-away & group bookings

    Most of the counter is available to take away. Coach parties and larger groups can be catered for — give us a ring in advance.
